About Jell-O

Jell-O is a delightful, colorful gelatin dessert loved for its smooth texture and sweet flavors. Made primarily from gelatin, Jell-O is sourced from collagen found in animal bones and skin, which is transformed into the iconic dessert we know and love.

Benefits of Jell-O

  • Low in calories, making it a great option for light desserts.
  • Provides a source of collagen, which can support healthy skin, joints, and hair.
  • A fun, refreshing treat that can be enjoyed on various occasions.

Fun Fact!

Did you know? Jell-O has been a popular dessert since the 19th century and was once called the “most famous dessert in America”!
